Introduction

Ila Sachani, hailing from Moti Vavdi village in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, is a remarkable artisan celebrated for her exquisite embroidery, all crafted using her feet.

Early Life and Challenges

Born with a congenital condition that left her arms unusable, Ila's journey is a testament to resilience and ingenuity. Under the guidance of her mother and grandmother, both adept in Kathiawar embroidery, Ila mastered the art of threading needles and creating intricate designs with her feet.
